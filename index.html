Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our organic henna process and booking details.

With proper aftercare, a natural henna stain typically lasts 1-2 weeks. The color usually peaks around 48 hours after application, turning into a rich, dark mahogany, and then gradually fades as your skin exfoliates.

Absolutely. We mix our own paste using 100% natural, triple-sifted henna powder, organic essential oils (like eucalyptus or lavender), sugar, and lemon juice. We never use "black henna" or chemical dyes that can harm the skin.

For bridal sessions, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially during the peak wedding season (May to September). For regular parties or individual appointments, 2-4 weeks is usually sufficient.

Please ensure the area to be hennaed is clean, dry, and free of any lotions or oils. We recommend gently exfoliating the area the day before to remove dead skin cells, which helps the stain penetrate deeper.
